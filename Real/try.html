2010 - The Parallax Revolution

The Parallax Effect revolutionized web design in 2010, offering a way to create depth and movement in web pages. By moving different layers of content at varying speeds, designers can craft immersive and dynamic experiences that engage users.

This technique uses multiple background images or layers that shift at different rates, simulating a 3D effect when users scroll through the page. It became a popular trend in web design, symbolizing innovation and creativity.
